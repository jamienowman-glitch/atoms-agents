## Card surface palette

- Base tokens:
  - `card_surface_dark`, `card_surface_light`
  - `card_stroke`
  - `card_focus_ring`
  - `card_glow_colour`
  - `card_overlay`
- Badge/flag background token for embedded markers.
- Keep defaults dark: surface=#0b0b0b, stroke=#ffffff, focus ring=#ffffff, glow=rgba(255,255,255,0.12).
- Provide inverted/light variant tokens for use on light themes.
