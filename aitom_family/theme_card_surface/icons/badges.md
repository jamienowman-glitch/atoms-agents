## Badge/flag icons

- Optional badge icon slot at card corner:
  - Default: none; allow `check`, `star`, or `flag` icons from /icons/ as swaps.
- Tokens:
  - `badge_icon_size`
  - `badge_position` (corner + offset respecting radius)
  - `badge_background_colour` (tie to palette)
  - `badge_icon_colour` (invert on light variant)
- Note [NO DEFAULT ICONS]; placement rules still apply for future overrides.
