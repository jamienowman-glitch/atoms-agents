## Text role colours

- `text_primary` – default white on black base.
- `text_secondary` – softer white/grey for supporting text.
- `text_muted` – lower emphasis meta.
- `text_error`, `text_success` – align with scheme status tokens.
- `text_link` – typically accent/white; ensure contrast on dark base.
- `text_disabled` – reduced opacity token.
- All colours reference scheme tokens; no hardcoded hex beyond defaults.
