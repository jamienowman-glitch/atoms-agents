## Typography presets

- Headings: `h1`–`h6` Roboto Flex. Default sizes: h1 32–36px desktop (scale down to 26px mobile), h2 28–32px, h3 24–28px, h4 20–22px, h5 18–20px, h6 16–18px.
- Body: `body_lg` (18–20px), `body_md` (16px), `body_sm` (14px).
- Labels/meta: `label`, `meta` (12–14px).
- Buttons: `button` preset with strong tracking and medium weight.
- Caption/overline: `caption` (12–13px) with uppercase optional.
- Each preset references axis tokens; colours set elsewhere.
