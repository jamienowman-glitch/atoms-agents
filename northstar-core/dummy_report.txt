Trends Report 2025

1. The Rise of "Slow Tech": People are moving away from constant notifications and seeking "dumb phones" and offline hobbies.
2. AI Agents in Daily Life: Personal assistants are becoming proactive, managing schedules and shopping without prompts.
3. Urban Farming: Hydroponics in small apartments is trending as food prices rise.
4. Retro Gaming Consoles: Nostalgia for N64 and PS1 era games is driving a hardware collection boom.
5. Python for Kids: Coding education is shifting to younger demographics using gamified Python interfaces.
